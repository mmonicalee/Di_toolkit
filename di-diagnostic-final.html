Decision Intelligence

How do you make decisions?

This tool is grounded in research on how individuals and organizations make decisions — drawing from fields including cognitive science, organizational behaviour, and leadership development.

It is designed as a reflection instrument, not a clinical assessment. The results are intended to help you think more clearly about your decision-making patterns — where your strengths lie and where there may be room to grow.

The research behind Decision Intelligence is ongoing, and this diagnostic will continue to evolve. Use it as a starting point for honest conversation, not as a definitive verdict.
